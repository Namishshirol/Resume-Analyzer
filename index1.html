    ⚠️ Common Mistakes to Avoid

    💼 General ATS Tips

    • Clear Sections: Use standard headings: Summary, Experience, Education, Skills, Projects, Certifications
    • Logical Flow: Arrange sections from most relevant to least relevant for the role
    • Consistent Structure: Keep all job entries in the same format for easy ATS parsing
    • Keywords First: Include 8-12 relevant keywords from job description in your resume
    • Action Verbs: Start bullets with power words: Led, Managed, Developed, Designed, Increased
    • Quantifiable Results: Show impact with numbers: "30% improvement", "managed 5-person team", "$50k cost reduction"
    • Tailor Content: Customize for each job by mirroring job description language
    • Standard Fonts: Use Arial, Calibri, or Times New Roman (11-12pt)
    • ATS-Friendly: No images, tables, colored text, special characters, or footer/header
    • Simple Layout: Avoid columns, text boxes, graphics, and complex formatting
    • Bullet Points: Use simple bullets (-) instead of fancy symbols
    • File Format: Submit as PDF or plain text, not Word documents (unless required)
    • Length Target: Keep to 1-2 pages max (prioritize last 10 years of experience)
    • Margins: Use 0.5-0.75" margins to fit more content efficiently
    • Test Your Resume: Copy/paste into text editor to verify formatting compatibility
    • Keyword Mirror: Use exact terminology from job posting (e.g., "Machine Learning" not just "AI")
